About me

During my bachelors, I’ve been participating in various projects as game developer, illustrator, game and narrative designer and storyboard artists. I was striving to craft pleasant and smooth experience for players, and ended up working as a full time UX designer shortly after graduation, where I was able to combine my coding, design and research skills. Besides that, I do different commercial illustrations from time to time, visual recordings of talks, and ceramics. You can find these works under the Play section :)
Currently within HCI, I’m trying to focus on emotional design, persuasive design and behavioral change. I try to prototype mostly with tangible and smart materials to create embedded interactions.
