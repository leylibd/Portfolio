Vase №1

  • Date: October 2021
  • Materials: white clay, engobe, transparent and brown glaze

Process

This is the first vase i created and overall my second (successful) work. I used strips-layering technique for that, and making strip shorter as i was approaching the narrow parts of the vase. After it slightly dried, i cut the leaves on it and painted it with green engobe. After it was biscuit fired, i applied transparent glaze outside, and brown glaze inside.
